Sole Proprietor CRM

Collaborated in a team of three and built a customer relationship management web application, designed for sole proprietors to manage leads and invoices.

The application provides a centralized hub for tracking customer interactions, managing sales pipelines, and generating professional invoices. Built with modern technologies for scalability and ease of use.

Project Purpose and Goal

My goal was to build a functional CRM that removes the friction between lead capture and revenue. I wanted a system that keeps client data centralized and secure.

Automates invoice generation and tracking. Offers a self-hosted, cost-effective alternative to SaaS tools like Pipedrive or HubSpot for small businesses.
