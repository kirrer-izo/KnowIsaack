Sole Proprietor CRM

Sole Proprietor CRM is a streamlined, efficient, and self-hosted Customer Relationship Management (CRM) tailored specifically for sole proprietors and freelancers.

Unlike bloated enterprise solutions, this CRM focuses on: organizing client interactions, tracking customer projects and tasks to be done by the business owner.

Project Purpose and Goal

My goal was to build a functional CRM meant for sole-proprietors and freelancers to manage their customers, tasks and projects.

The result is a lightweight platform that manages customers, tasks and projects.

MODULES OVERVIEW

Dashboard Overview

A clean dashboard that provides at-a-glance insights into the user's business metrics, recent activities, and pending tasks. All critical information is organized for quick access.

OPERATIONAL AWARENESS

The Analysis Center

Start every day with clarity. The analysis module aggregates real-time data of all customers, active projects, and tasks. It gives freelancers an immediate snapshot of their business health without digging through database rows.
