Live Your Books

Live Your Books is a self-improvement web application that helps users apply the lessons learned from books into their daily lives through interactive journaling and progress tracking.

Live Your Books

Live Your Books leverages artificial intelligence to stimulate mentorship from renowned thinkers and authors, guiding users towards continuous self-improvement. The project aims to bridge the gap between passive reading and the meaningful, real world application of knowledge.

The Modules are namely; Books, Task Generator, Habit Tracker, Journaling, AI Mentor, and AI Features (which includes: weekly growth summary, personalized advice, habit suggestions, and routine builder).

Project Purpose and Goal

My goal was to develop a self-improvement web application that helps users apply lessons from books into their daily lives through journaling and progress tracking.

I intended to bridge the gap between theory and practice, through modules like the AI Mentor that enables users to chat with the AI on the selected book and the AI would emulate the persona of the selected mentor. There is also the AI Features module which enhances the user journey through personalized tasks tailored to the user.

MODULES OVERVIEW

Dashboard Overview

On the Dashboard the user is able to see an overview of all modules. The user can monitor a list of all books, active tasks, habits and journal entries. There are also sections displaying the five most recent items of books, habits and recent tasks.

DATA DRIVEN INSIGHTS

AI Features

Turn raw activity into actionable direction. The AI analyses the users tasks, habits, books and journals to generate weekly progress and personalized advice. The user selects the book or mentor the AI should emulate and provide their intended goals and waits for the AI to generate habits or personalized advice.

Available Features

  • Weekly Progress
  • Personalized Advice
  • Habit Suggestion
  • Routine Builder
