MOTIVATION

Why I Built This

Like most people my age, I found it much easier to spend hours on social media than twenty minutes reading a book. Social media is designed to be frictionless and addictive. Books, while valuable, often feel like work.

I realised the problem wasn't the books — it was the delivery. I set out to build an experience as engaging as the apps we can't put down. This became my final-year project at Kabarak University.

APPROACH

The Solution

I borrowed the habit-forming patterns of modern apps and applied them to self-improvement — three core ideas drove the design:

  1. Social-style Dashboard Your feed shows active habits, recent tasks, and reading insights instead of strangers' posts.
  2. AI Mentors via Gemini API Chat with the persona of any author as easily as sending a DM.
  3. Habit Tracker with Streaks Complete a book-derived task and get the same dopamine hit as a "like" — with actual personal growth attached.
MODULE 01 — DASHBOARD

A high-focus, low-friction interface built to surface what matters most — your progress, habits, and reading activity — the moment you log in.

  • Growth Metrics Total Books, Active Tasks, Active Habits and Journal Entries visible at a glance
  • Real-time Data React frontend pulls live data from a Laravel backend on every load
  • Streak Tracking Visual progress cues keep habits consistent and completion rewarding

MODULE 02 — AI MENTOR

Talk to Your Books

Instead of highlighting a passage in Atomic Habits, I wanted to ask James Clear directly — "How do I apply Chapter 1 to my schedule today?" The AI Mentor Chat turns reading into a conversation.

The Hard Part

Preventing the AI from hallucinating or breaking character. I refined system-level prompts and output constraints so that when you're talking to a Stoic philosopher, the tone, vocabulary, and advice stays authentically Stoic — not generic ChatGPT.

MODULE 03 — AI FEATURES

Your Personal Growth Coach

A feature that connects the dots between the books you're reading and the life you're actually living — surfacing insights, habits, and advice that are specific to you.

The Hard Part

Data Synthesis. I had to architect the Laravel backend to aggregate tasks, habits, and journal entries into a structured context payload — feeding the right information into Gemini API without overwhelming its context window.

What It Does

  • Weekly Progress Report Data-driven snapshot of your habits, tasks and reading evolution
  • Personalized Advice AI frames guidance through your chosen persona — Stoic, Buddhist, or others
  • Habit Suggestions App suggests habits based on books you're currently reading
  • Routine Builder Turns book-derived tasks into a structured daily routine
MODULE 04 — ENGINEERING

Technical Execution

The decisions made under the hood that made the product feel alive.

01

Architecting the Core

Built a Laravel backend capable of handling complex relationships between book modules, journals, and habit tracking. Implemented caching layers for frequently accessed data like habit streaks — reducing database latency and keeping the experience frictionless.

Laravel Caching MySQL
02

AI Integration & Prompt Engineering

Integrating Gemini API was about more than API calls — it was about maintaining authentic personas. Designed strict system-level constraints to minimise hallucinations and keep the Mentor grounded in the specific book being discussed.

Gemini API Prompt Engineering
03

Resource Optimisation

Avoided heavy full-file uploads by developing a name-input system. This reduced storage usage while maintaining enough metadata for the AI to provide highly relevant, content-aware responses.

Storage Metadata
04

Security by Design

This app handles deeply personal journal entries and growth data. Built with privacy at the core — all sensitive user information and passwords are encrypted at rest within the database.
